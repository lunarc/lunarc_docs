# Creating a password for your account

!!! info "Prerequisites"
    Before creating a password you must have:

    1. A [SUPR](https://supr.naiss.se/person/) account with a registered mobile phone number
    2. An approved project membership on a LUNARC resource
    3. An active LUNARC account — apply for one under the **Accounts** section of your SUPR profile once your project membership is approved

## Steps

### 1. Go to the password self-service portal

[Password self-service portal](https://phenix3.lunarc.lu.se/pss){ .md-button }

### 2. Identify yourself

Enter your email address. No existing password is required. The system will send a one-time password (OTP) by SMS to the mobile phone registered in your SUPR profile.

### 3. Set your password

Enter the OTP when prompted and choose your new password. The change takes effect immediately.

!!! warning "Getting a 'Check your details to complete the login' error?"
    This usually means your LUNARC account has not been created yet. Verify that:

    - Your project membership in SUPR has been approved
    - You have applied for a LUNARC account under the **Accounts** section of your [SUPR profile](https://supr.naiss.se/person/)

!!! tip "Also setting up two-factor authentication?"
    Once your password is set, check that you have an [active Pocket Pass token](authenticator_howto.md#checking-the-validity-of-your-token) before trying to log in — you will need it.

## Need further help?

If the portal does not work, call LUNARC support on **+46 (0)46-222 4454** from your registered mobile phone. Additional questions will be asked to verify your identity.
